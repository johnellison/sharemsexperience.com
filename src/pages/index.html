Do you or a loved one have MS?

Would you be willing to share your experiences?

Several of our close family members suffer from MS and other autoimmune conditions. We want to better understand how we might be able to help them and possibly others. So we've decided to conduct an informal research study to learn more about how people with MS live their lives.

This short video explains a bit more about our research with some background about where we're coming from and why we're so motivated to help people with MS.

How It Works

Step 1

Fill out a 5-minute form to express your interest. Decide whether you want to share your experiences:

  • In-person (at a location of your choosing)
  • Over the phone (or Skype)
  • Through a 15-minute online form

Step 2

We get in touch regarding next steps.

Step 3

You share your experiences.

Step 4

We follow-up with a huge thank you and let you know about the outcomes of the research.
