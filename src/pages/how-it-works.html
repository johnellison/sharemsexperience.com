How the application process works

A brief overview before you start your application.

Step 1

You fill out a secure 3-5 minute application to tell us a bit about yourself and provide a way to contact you.

Step 2

We get in touch to talk about the next step and provide a way for you to schedule a discussion at your convenience.

Step 3

You will have a chat with one of our team members (John, Sophie or Lucy), usually lasting around 30 minutes.

Everything you share is 100% confidential. You can stop the discussion at any time.

Step 4

We follow-up with a huge thank you. We'll take some time to reflect upon the information we gathered from you and others.

If you'd like to be informed, we will follow-up with an email describing the outcomes of the study and providing ways to stay in touch.
