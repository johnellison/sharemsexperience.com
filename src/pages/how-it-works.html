How the application process works

A brief overview before you start your application.

Step 1

You fill out a secure 3-5 minute application to tell us a bit about yourself and provide a way to contact you.

Step 2

We get in touch to talk about next steps and put a date in the diary to hear about your experience.

Step 3

You will have a discussion with John or Sophie, usually lasting around 30 minutes.

Everything you share is 100% confidential. You can stop the discussion at any time.

Step 4

We follow-up with a huge thank you. We'll take some time to analyze the information we gathered and decide on next steps.

If you'd like to be informed, we will follow-up with an email describing the outcomes of the study.
